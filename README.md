# Kidnap & Ransom Exposure & Underwriting Intelligence Platform

A professional, self-contained underwriting intelligence dashboard designed to help insurers, brokers, risk consultants, and exposure specialists evaluate Kidnap & Ransom (K&R) risk through interactive portfolio analytics, risk modelling, and underwriting simulations.

Built as a single-page application with no external infrastructure requirements, the platform demonstrates how data-driven underwriting can be used to assess exposure, support pricing decisions, and identify risk mitigation opportunities in high-threat operating environments.

---

## Executive Summary

The Kidnap & Ransom Exposure & Underwriting Intelligence Platform provides a comprehensive view of portfolio risk by combining geopolitical volatility, travel exposure, industry threat profiles, historical incident data, and client security controls into an interactive underwriting experience.

The solution enables users to:

- Assess portfolio-wide exposure trends
- Simulate underwriting scenarios in real time
- Compare sector-specific threat levels
- Evaluate the effectiveness of mitigation controls
- Explore individual policy risk characteristics
- Support premium and retention decision-making

A pre-generated synthetic portfolio containing 100 policies is included to provide immediate analytics and visualisation capabilities without requiring external datasets.

---

## Key Features

### Executive Dashboard

Gain instant visibility across the portfolio through a collection of real-time analytics and visual insights, including:

- Total portfolio exposure metrics
- Risk distribution across underwriting bands
- Average premium and retention analysis
- High-risk policy identification
- Interactive risk score versus premium visualisation
- Severity-weighted portfolio comparisons

---

### Underwriting Risk Simulator

A dynamic underwriting engine allowing users to model the impact of security controls, operational behaviour, and regional threats on policy risk.

Simulated factors include:

- Close Protection Services
- Crisis Response Retainers
- HEAT (Hostile Environment Awareness Training)
- Travel Tracking Capabilities
- Historical Incident Experience
- Travel Frequency
- Regional Instability
- Sector Risk Profile

Outputs include:

- Dynamic Risk Score
- Estimated Premium
- Recommended Retention
- Sector Benchmark Comparison
- Underwriting Recommendations
- Suggested Coverage Restrictions or Exclusions

---

### Portfolio Explorer

A searchable and filterable portfolio management view providing:

- Advanced sorting and filtering
- Policy-level drill-down capability
- Risk profile comparison
- Exposure concentration analysis
- Premium and retention transparency

Designed to replicate the workflow of an underwriting portfolio review.

---

### Sector Heat Intelligence

Visualise relative exposure across sectors through an interactive heat map displaying:

- Average Risk Scores
- Incident Probability
- Claim Severity
- Average Premium
- Base Threat Indicators

Colour-coded risk levels allow rapid identification of sectors requiring enhanced underwriting scrutiny.

---

### Threat Exposure Analytics

Explore risk drivers across industries with intelligence focused on:

- Incident likelihood
- Average ransom demand
- Crisis response costs
- Average captivity duration
- International travel exposure
- Regional threat concentration

This view supports a deeper understanding of underlying exposure drivers and loss potential.

## Portfolio Model

The included portfolio consists of 100 synthetic policies generated using weighted risk algorithms that incorporate:

- Sector exposure
- Travel frequency
- Security control maturity
- Regional instability
- Historical incident activity
- Threat probability modelling

This provides realistic underwriting scenarios while ensuring no real client information is utilised.

## Disclaimer

**Important:** All data presented within this platform is entirely synthetic and generated for demonstration purposes.

Risk scores, premiums, ransom estimates, loss costs, threat indicators, captivity durations, underwriting assumptions, and portfolio characteristics are fictional models designed to showcase analytical capabilities and user experience design.

The platform does **not** represent:

- Real insured entities
- Actual policy data
- Proprietary underwriting models
- Client portfolios
- Claims records
- Market pricing methodologies

It should be used solely as a demonstration, educational, or proof-of-concept application.
